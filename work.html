Case Study #1 - The Downing Bottles & Bites

The Downing Bottles & Bites is a local restaurant located in Rockwall, Texas. They reached out in hopes to rebrand the company by retaining their modern style, but still giving an uplifting vibe to the company.

I created several versions that had the minimalist style while still keeping their vintage modern feel, but the one displayed to the left triumphed all. The cursive typography paired with the sans font gave a great illustration of their brand identity.

Case Study #2 - Velez Cleaning Solutions

Velez Cleaning Solutions reached out to me to seek help with a full branding experience. I was called to create a new logo, stationary assets, and website design

Case Study #4 - Auto Detail Los Angeles

Auto Detail Los Angeles was a big project, as we were called to create a new brand from the ground up, including the brand name. It was vital for research within the Los Angeles community, while trying to organize the values of the business.

Auto Detail Los Angeles wanted to exhibit class, luxury, efficiency, and high attention to detail. It was important that we created something sleek and bold, while still connecting with the values of the brand.
